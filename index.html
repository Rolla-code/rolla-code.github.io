About

Engineering with real-world business awareness

I'm a results-driven full-stack .NET developer based in Accra, Ghana, with a track record of designing and delivering scalable financial and enterprise applications for teams in Ghana and North Macedonia. For the past several years at Process Crafting Expert Services, I've led backend and integration work on multinational financial platforms — real-time transaction processing, cashbox workflows, and third-party fintech integrations.

I specialize in backend architecture, API integrations, and cloud-ready systems, and I'm equally at home building polished, state-managed front ends with React and Tailwind CSS.
